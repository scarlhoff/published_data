2015_RaghavanScience

Package version: 2.0.0 (2022-01-28), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Description: Diachronic transect of ancient individuals from the Americas. This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2)

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de)

Literature: Raghavan et al. (2015)

Raghavan, M., M. Steinrucken, K. Harris, S. Schiffels, S. Rasmussen, M. DeGiorgio, A. Albrechtsen, et al. 2015. “Genomic Evidence for the Pleistocene and Recent Population History of Native Americans.” Science 349 (6250): aab3884–aab3884. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aab3884.
