Reference_Genomes

Package version: 2.0.0 (2022-01-28), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Description: Genetic data for Reference Genomes

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de), Clemens Schmid (schmid@shh.mpg.de)

Literature: Church et al. (2011), 1000 Genomes Project Consortium et al. (2015), Waterson et al. (2005), Gordon et al. (2016)

1000 Genomes Project Consortium, Adam Auton, Lisa D Brooks, Richard M Durbin, Erik P Garrison, Hyun Min Kang, Jan O Korbel, et al. 2015. “A Global Reference for Human Genetic Variation.” Nature 526 (7571): 68–74. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature15393.

Church, Deanna M, Valerie A Schneider, Tina Graves, Katherine Auger, Fiona Cunningham, Nathan Bouk, Hsiu-Chuan Chen, et al. 2011. “Modernizing Reference Genome Assemblies.” PLoS Biol. 9 (7): e1001091. https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pbio.1001091.

Gordon, David, John Huddleston, Mark J P Chaisson, Christopher M Hill, Zev N Kronenberg, Katherine M Munson, Maika Malig, et al. 2016. “Long-Read Sequence Assembly of the Gorilla Genome.” Science 352 (6281): aae0344. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aae0344.

Waterson, Robert H., Eric S. Lander, Richard K. Wilson, The Chimpanzee Sequencing, and Analysis Consortium. 2005. “Initial Sequence of the Chimpanzee Genome and Comparison with the Human Genome.” Nature 437 (7055): 69–87. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature04072.
