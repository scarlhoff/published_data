Package version: 2.0.1 (2022-02-07), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Changelog:

  • V 2.0.1: Genetic_Source_Accession_IDs added
  • V 2.0.0: updated the package to Poseidon v2.5 and adjusted the .janno file accordingly

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de)

Description: Mesolithic and Neolithic samples from Sweden and Gotland. This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2)

Literature: Skoglund et al. (2014)

Skoglund, P., H. Malmstrom, A. Omrak, M. Raghavan, C. Valdiosera, T. Gunther, P. Hall, et al. 2014. “Genomic Diversity and Admixture Differs for Stone-Age Scandinavian Foragers and Farmers.” Science 344 (6185): 747–50. https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1253448.
