2018_OlaldeNature

Package version: 2.0.0 (2022-01-28), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Description: This package was initially constructed based on the Allen Ancient DNA Resource (https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data, version 37.2). Ancient genomes from the Bell Beaker period in Europe.

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de)

Literature: Olalde et al. (2018)

Olalde, Iñigo, Selina Brace, Morten E. Allentoft, Ian Armit, Kristian Kristiansen, Thomas Booth, Nadin Rohland, et al. 2018. “The Beaker Phenomenon and the Genomic Transformation of Northwest Europe.” Nature 555 (7695): 190–96. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature25738.
