Archaic_Humans

Package version: 2.0.0 (2022-01-28), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Description: Genetic data of Archaic Humans

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de)

Literature: Prüfer et al. (2017), Prüfer et al. (2013), Meyer et al. (2012), Slon et al. (2018), Hajdinjak et al. (2018)
