2020_Fernandes_Caribbean

Package version: 2.0.1 (2022-03-13), Poseidon version: 2.5.0

Description: Diachronic collection of samples from the pre-contact Caribbean.

Contributors: Ayshin Ghalichi (ghalichi@shh.mpg.de), Clemens Schmid (schmid@shh.mpg.de)

Literature: Fernandes et al. (2021)

Fernandes, Daniel M., Kendra A. Sirak, Harald Ringbauer, Jakob Sedig, Nadin Rohland, Olivia Cheronet, Matthew Mah, et al. 2021. “A Genetic History of the Pre-Contact Caribbean.” Nature 590 (7844): 103–10. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-03053-2.
